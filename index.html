Barangay Puguis

Barangay Puguis is one of the largest barangay in the municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet. Puguis is a home of the Ibalois, they originally founded in the valley particularly Puguis and Pico. The District of Benguet was created at Pukis(Puguis) and La Trinidad was made by the "Commandancia Politico Militares de Benguet".

PROGRAMS

The barangay actively established programs for the welfare of its citizens.

"Bata Bata Magensayo Ka"

This program is about the youth encouragement to be socially involved. The program, "Bata Bata Magensayo Ka" was inpired by the precious incident in the school barangay where three of their students was teased and bullied. They were threatend to give money, thus the barangay officials and concern citizens in the barangay proposed a program to encourage "out of school youth" to participate in a Basketball: Liga. Through the said program, a lot of youth turned their habits and vices to playing basketball. The barangay is proud to say, "Incident's like before is now minimal".

The coach of the basketball must be very strict to them, for the youth to be discipline and they should know how to manage their time. It also help them to be matured enough for them to be motivated and for them to be challenge.

Information Education Campaign (IEC)

This program is being conducted inside the barangay school. The program encourages students to be studious and participate in the schools activities. By the said purpose of the program, everybody is encourage to go to school. As humans, everyone has the right to learn.

Especially to the minors who committed crimes out of curiosity. They are being taught in a way they'll redirect into a good pathway.We live in a place where education is valued and is a privilege to everyone

POLICIES

"A right delayed is a right denied"

- Martin Luther King

Puguis, one of the barangay in the La Trinidad, Benguet is strictly imposing human right laws. All of the human rights policies were taken from the Philippine Laws regarding the Human Rights of each individual.

Human Rights

Some basic human rights are:

  1. Right of life
  2. Right of education
  3. Freedom of thought, conscience and religion
  4. Freedom of movement
  5. No person should be imprisoned for debt or non-payment of a poll tax
